What is pest control

Pest control is the process of managing and controlling species that threaten New Zealand's native wildlife and ecosystems. Proper pest control helps protect our birds. forests, waterways and human health.

Common pests

Control Methods

New Zealand faces challenges from pests such as rats, stoats, possums and feral cats. These animals threaten native species and damage our natural habitats

Community pest control programmes and predator free solutions help restore our native ecosystems and support kaitiakitanga by caring for our environment
